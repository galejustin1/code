Nominations for the 2021 Oticon Focus on People Awards are open!


The Oticon Focus on People Awards celebrate students, adults, advocacy volunteers and hearing health professionals who are changing perceptions of what it means to have a hearing loss and opening doors of opportunity for all.

Your nomination could change someone’s life. In addition to national recognition, Oticon Focus on People Award winners will receive a cash prize, a donation to the charity of their choice and Oticon hearing aids with BrainHearing™ technology, like the new Oticon More™.
